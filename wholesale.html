Wholesale

The wholesale side of Styles Farmhouse Ice Cream has grown form strength to strength over the past few years with us not only delivering across Somerset and parts of Devon but also across the South West with the help of external distributors.

If you own your own business or the business you work for are looking for an ice cream provider please do not hesitate to get in touch. We are more than happy to come and see you with samples and further information about what we can offer you and why coming on board with Styles can help expand your business.

Why choose Styles... We offer
  • a variety of products and services with both scooped or in-house for desserts
  • our own brand of soft mix
  • a range of different size cones and the option of standard or luxury
  • a number of Wall’s popular lollies and of course Cadbury’s Flakes
  • we offer all the point of sale including flavour boards, pavement stands and flags
  • the flavour boards can be made up to your own specifications
  • we will do all we can to help ensure the transition is easy and successful for you

If you would like to arrange for one of our Wholesale representatives to come and see you or if you have any questions at all, please either call the office on 01984 640255 or email them at wholesale@styles-icecream.co.uk
